3. Data Collection

Our website is purely informational and static. We do not collect any personal data directly from users. However, technical data such as IP addresses may be collected automatically by the hosting service provider GitHub Pages.

  • Referrer URL

This data cannot be assigned to specific individuals and is not merged with other data sources. We reserve the right to check this data retrospectively if there are concrete indications of illegal use.

Gallery and Image Publication

In our gallery, we publish images taken in various contexts. To ensure that we respect privacy and the rights of depicted individuals, we follow these guidelines:
  • Consent to Image Publication: We have obtained explicit consent from all individuals depicted in the images for their publication. This consent covers the use of images on our website and in other media related to our activities.
  • Blurring and Anonymization: If individuals are visible in the images who have not given consent for publication or whose consent cannot be verified, these images will be edited to obscure or blur the affected individuals.
  • Right to Withdraw: If you, as a depicted individual, wish to withdraw your consent for the publication of images, please contact us immediately. We will promptly take action to remove or edit the relevant images.
